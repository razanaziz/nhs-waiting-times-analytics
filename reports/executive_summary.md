# Executive Summary

## Problem
NHS emergency departments have experienced increasing waiting times due to operational pressures, workforce shortages, and rising patient demand.

## Objective
This project investigated the factors contributing to prolonged A&E waiting times using statistical analysis and forecasting techniques.

## Methods
- Regression analysis
- Correlation analysis
- SARIMA forecasting
- Operational healthcare analytics

## Key Findings
- Bed occupancy significantly impacted delays
- Workforce shortages worsened performance
- Waiting times are forecasted to continue increasing without intervention

## Recommendations
- Improve workforce planning
- Reduce bed occupancy pressure
- Expand predictive operational monitoring
- Improve patient discharge efficiency
